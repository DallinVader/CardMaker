* RULES
   * Game Setup
• Each player shuffles their deck and draws 8 cards.
• Each player places their Commander into play before the game begins.
• The player who lost the previous game goes first. Or if this is the first round flip a coin 
   * Decks and Drawing
• Draw 1 card at the start of your turn.
• Decks must contain at least 60 cards and a maximum of 100 cards. and no more than 4 copies of the same card.
• Cards may be played during your turn unless stated otherwise.
• If you search your deck, shuffle it afterward.
• Discarded cards are placed face up in a Discard Pile that any player may view.
   * Treasury
• The Treasury stores your Wealth cards and is placed behind your cards in play.
• You may convert 1 Wealth card from your hand to the Treasury each turn.
• Spend 4 Wealth to draw 1 card.
• Some cards may generate additional Wealth.
   * Commanders (needs editing/clarification)
• Your Commander is your leader. If its Defense reaches 0, you lose the game.
• Commanders recover to full Defense at the start of your turn.
• Commanders can be targeted unless a card says otherwise.
• Each player may only have one Commander in play.
   * Attack and Defense
• Cards may have Attack (left) and Defense (right).
• Attack deals damage to another card’s Defense.
• When Defense reaches 0, the card is discarded.
• Defense does not automatically reset unless stated.
• Track lost Defense using dice or tokens.
• Commanders are the only cards that recover Defense automatically each turn.
   * Exhausted
• At the start of your turn, ready all your Exhausted cards by turning them upright.
• Exhausted cards cannot Protect, Assault, or use activated effects.
• Exhausted cards are turned sideways.
• Exhausted Units defend at half damage (rounded up).
   * Units
• Units are placed on the battlefield in front of your Treasury.
• Units cannot Assault the turn they enter play.
• Units may defend even while Exhausted.
• Exhausted Units deal half damage (rounded up) when defending.
• Units are discarded when their Defense reaches 0.
   * Protecting
• Ready Units with Attack or Defense may Protect another card with Attack or Defense.
• The protecting Unit takes the damage instead and becomes Exhausted.
   * Assaulting
• Units may Assault Units, Holdings, Commanders, or other cards with Defense.
• The attacker deals damage equal to its Attack.
• The attacker also takes damage equal to the defender’s Attack.
• After Assaulting, the Unit becomes Exhausted.
   * Spells
• Spells resolve immediately and are then discarded.
• Spells that say “Play at any time” may be played during any player’s turn.
• Targets must be chosen when the spell is played.
   * Equipment
• Equipment attaches to a Unit or Holding.
• Multiple Equipment may be attached unless stated otherwise.
• If the attached card leaves play, the Equipment is discarded.
   * Enchantments
• Enchantments attach to a card and remain until destroyed or the card leaves play.
• Multiple Enchantments may be attached unless stated otherwise.
   * Tactics
• Tactics give global effects to your cards.
• You may only have 1 Tactic in play at a time.
• Playing a new Tactic replaces the old one.
   * Holdings
• Spend 1 Wealth to add a Unit to a Holding.
• Holdings may contain any number of Units unless stated otherwise.
• Units inside may defend the Holding.
• When a Holding is defeated, the attacker may discard or capture it. Captured Holdings should remain facing the original owner to avoid mixing cards.
   * Attachments
• Equipment and Enchantments are attachments.
• Attachments remain until destroyed or the attached card leaves play.
   * Death Effects
• Effects triggered when a card is killed resolve before the card enters the Discard Pile.
• If multiple effects trigger, the active player resolves theirs first.
   * Targeting
• Cards that require a target must choose a valid one when played.
• If the target becomes invalid before resolution, the effect fails.
